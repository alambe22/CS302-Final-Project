Unit details that will be implementated into the game

SNIPER
-Movement:can only move one tile in any direction including diagonal
-Attack: single target only
-Health: 2500
-Damage: 550
-Range:  up to 10 tiles

ARTILLERY
-Movement: move 3 tiles in adjacent direction only and only foward
-Attack: splash damage in a 2x2 area (starting from top left)
-Health: 6000
-Damage: 250 to each tile in the 2x2 area
-Range: 2x2 area only a tile away

INFANTRY
-Movement: 1 tile 
-Attack: Only diagonal tiles (forward and backward)
-Health: 3000
-Damage: 1100
-Range: 1 tile diagonal from its current position

CAVALRY
-Movement: up to  2 tile in any adjacent directon
-Attack: can damage any unit that is in the same row cavalry is in
-Health: 4000
-Damage: 375 to each unit in that row
-Range: length of row
